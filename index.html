profitAlgo

profitAlgo is an algorithm that attempts to convert your proficiency-based (1 to 4) grade into a percentage.

Note: This algorithm assumes that a grade of 1 is worth 0%, a 2 is worth 35.5% (the average of 1% and 70%, anything between the grade of 1 and 3), a 3 is worth 70% (a passing grade), 3.5 is 100%, and 4 is worth 105%.
